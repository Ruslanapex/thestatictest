    Questions people actually ask

    Is this a replacement for therapy?

    No, and it's not pretending to be. It's a practical starting point for people who want to move without booking an appointment or explaining themselves to anyone first. If what you're feeling goes deeper than direction, talking to a professional is worth it — this just isn't gatekept behind that.

    Why is the quiz free?

    Because a label alone isn't worth paying for. The quiz and the free breakdown are genuinely useful on their own — the $19 Roadmap is for people who want the full plan, not just the diagnosis.

    What if I get a different result if I retake it?

    That happens, and it's fine — most people sit close to two patterns at once. Go with whichever result actually made you go "oh." That reaction is more reliable than the exact score.

    Is my quiz data stored anywhere?

    No email or account is required to see your result, and your answers aren't stored or sent anywhere — the whole quiz runs in your browser.
